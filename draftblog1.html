Obviously I most often eat all the basics: butter chicken, garlic naan, etc. But recently I stopped at an Indian restaraunt that had a buffet going, and I got those things along with medu vada, samosa chat, paneer kadai, and gobi mutter, and they were all fantastic. I'd highly recommend any of these to seem less white.
